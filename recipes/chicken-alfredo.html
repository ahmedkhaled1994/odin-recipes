Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a rich and creamy pasta dish made with fettuccine, chicken, and a delicious Alfredo sauce. It's a comforting meal that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until golden brown and cooked through.
  3. Add the garlic to the skillet and cook for another minute.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately.
