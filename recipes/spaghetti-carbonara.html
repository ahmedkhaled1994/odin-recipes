Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's a simple yet delicious meal that can be prepared quickly.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, beat the eggs in a bowl, then finely grate in the Pecorino and Parmesan cheese and mix well.
  3. Fry the pancetta with the garlic in the butter until crispy.
  4. Remove the garlic and add the cooked spaghetti to the pancetta. Mix well.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture. Stir well to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
